Issue Time in Status

Easily find the time spent in the current status for the issue being viewed

Features

  • No need for setup as the information is displayed immediately on the right panel along with standard Jira properties
  • If you click over the glance, you can get the time spent on other statuses too

Few more details

The app measures the time each issue has been on a specific Status. The app measures even the previous duration the issue has been on a specific status. For instance, if initially the issue has been for 1 minute in TODO, then you move it to DOING, then you move it again to TODO. After a minute, it will report as 2 minutes spent in TODO status.
By default, the app is installed as a glance that gets displayed in the right side of the issue being viewed. It will show the total time the issue has spent on the current status.
If you click over the glance, it will show the total time the issue has spent in all the different statuses it has been before.
The calculated time is measured in hours.
